The Power Behind SABARICS

SABARICS was created by MASTER MASK, whose real name is MAC MARKO MASS. He is the second king of the MAS RAK Kingdom, the owner of MASS Industries, and the hero who made SABARICS known as one of the most powerful teams in the SAK Universe.

01

The Four Core Members

SABARICS has only four official members: MASTER MASK, IC, GOD, and RACULA. Together, they stand as the main force of the universe and enter the greatest wars when the world needs its strongest protectors.

02

War Alliance

Whenever SABARICS goes to war, IC's team, ANI POWS, joins them. GOD's team, the Circle Agents, also fights beside SABARICS, bringing more than 100,000 soldiers under GOD's command.

03

Kiya, The Right Hand

The whole SABARICS team is maintained by KIYA, the right hand of MASTER MASK. KIYA is also the CEO of MASS Industries, guiding operations, planning strategies, and giving powerful ideas to the team.

04

MASK AI

SABARICS also has an advanced AI named MASK. MASK was created by MASTER MASK and KIYA to support missions, protect intelligence, and help the team make stronger decisions during dangerous battles.

05

How GOD and RACULA Joined

GOD joined first when Master Mask offered him a place in Sabarics without asking him to leave the Circle Agents. He became the team’s third member and its shield. Racula joined later, after Master Mask and the team stopped Matt’s rampage at the laboratory. Mac saw the brilliant scientist beneath the monster, promised to find a cure, and offered him a new purpose. Matt accepted, hid his former identity, and became the Dark Hero Racula — Sabarics’ fourth member.

06

Government Shadow

The team’s alliance is also a response to the Seven Governments. Their hidden hero programs, manipulation, and control of powered people make Sabarics necessary. GOD trained government forces in the past, while Racula knows how government secrets create villains. Together with Master Mask and IC, they choose to protect people on their own terms.
