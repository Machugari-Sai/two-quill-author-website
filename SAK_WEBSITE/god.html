The Story of GOD

Before he became the third member of Sabarics, Gati was a warrior who earned the name GOD through courage, sacrifice, and a bond with the lightning mace.

01

When Gati Became GOD

During the Great Battle, the Grand Master fought five enemies while the city burned. Gati saw one of them attacking civilians and stood between them. In the storm, lightning struck his raised mace. Instead of destroying him, the energy merged with the weapon and connected to his soul. The villagers who witnessed the miracle began calling him GOD, and the name remained.

02

Leader of the Circle Agents

Gati built the Circle Agents into a family of trained guards and fighters, many of them orphans who had lost their parents in hero battles. He leads them from their sky headquarters and protects them as fiercely as he protects the world.

03

How GOD Joined Sabarics

Master Mask asked Gati to join, but Gati first refused because the Circle Agents were his responsibility. Mac offered a compromise: Gati would remain their leader and answer Sabarics calls when the world truly needed him. Gati accepted the blue emergency watch, becoming Sabarics’ third member without abandoning his family.
