RACULA

The Dark Hero
4th Member of Sabarics

Real name Matt. Once the world’s greatest scientist, Matt became Racula after betrayal, poison, and an unfinished serum transformed him into something no longer human.

The Story of RACULA

His darkness was born in a laboratory, but his place in Sabarics began when Master Mask offered him a second life.

01

The Serum and the Monster

Matt created a serum meant to help humans survive space and deep water. His partner Tannie secretly mixed it with a poisonous formula and injected both into him. Matt answered with an unfinished emergency serum. The reaction changed his body: shadows surrounded him, his eyes turned crimson, bullets passed through him, and rage overwhelmed his mind. He killed the guards and Tannie before the alarm brought Sabarics to the laboratory.

02

Defeated by Sabarics

IC and GOD could not touch the transformed Matt, but Master Mask’s ancient weapon, the Vel, could. Mac struck him down and brought him to the Sabarics tower. After the team learned the truth, Mac recognized the scientist beneath the creature and promised to find a cure.

03

How Racula Joined

Matt wanted to die, but Master Mask offered him a purpose instead: join Sabarics and help people. Matt accepted on one condition — the world would believe that Matt and Tannie died in the laboratory explosion. His son had loved an old Dracula series, so Mac gave him a new name: the Dark Hero Racula. He became Sabarics’ fourth member.
