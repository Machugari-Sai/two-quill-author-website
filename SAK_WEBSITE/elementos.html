SAKYAMaster of Elementos

"Elementos are not subjects to be controlled, but truths to be understood."

Sakya is the legendary master who created the Elementos order. A neutral guiding force of unknown age and origin, he can harmonize and manipulate every element. His wisdom transcends time, and he trains each chosen hero to master both power and purpose.
