Description

In a world divided by ancient kingdoms, secret governments, powerful organizations, and forgotten legends, a new era is about to begin.

Mac Marko Mass appears to be nothing more than the brilliant owner of MASS Industries. But hidden beneath his ordinary life lies a destiny connected to the ancient kingdom of MasRak. After surviving betrayal, forbidden experiments, and unimaginable loss, he rises as Master Mask, determined to rebuild the legendary Sabarics and protect a world standing on the edge of chaos.

As dangerous enemies seek unimaginable power, new heroes emerge, each carrying their own past, abilities, and burdens. Ancient weapons awaken, mysterious powers reveal themselves, and friendships are tested through sacrifice, loyalty, and war. From the disciplined ANI POWs to the unstoppable Legon Star, from the mysterious Racula to the Seven Governments controlling the world from the shadows, every path leads toward a conflict far greater than anyone imagined.

Filled with breathtaking battles, emotional bonds, hidden mysteries, advanced technology, and an expansive fantasy universe, SAK: THE BEGINNING is the first chapter of an epic saga where heroes are forged, legends are born, and every choice shapes the future.
