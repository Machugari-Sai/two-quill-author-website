Book 02

SAK-2 The Flames of War

The war has begun. There is no turning back.

Description

Two years after the events of SAK: The Beginning, peace has become nothing more than a distant memory.

As the Seven Governments tighten their control over the world, ancient kingdoms rise from the shadows, forgotten heroes answer the call, and powerful Elementals awaken. Standing against the darkness is Master Mask, leader of the newly formed Sabarics, joined by Lebyote, God, IC, Racula, Evo, Zalax, Revox, and warriors bound by friendship, loyalty, and sacrifice.

But their greatest enemy is no ordinary tyrant. Yan has built an army of elite soldiers and terrifying creations capable of destroying kingdoms and rewriting history itself. As war engulfs Zytora, friendships are tested, sacrifices are made, and legends are forged in fire.

Every war demands a sacrifice.
